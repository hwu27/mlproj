Let's do it!

Teach and learn at the same time!

By utilizing "peer teaching" or "peer tutoring", you are able to teach others what you know and further your understanding as the same time.  By going deeper into your ML projects, you can really understand the specifics of your work. 

Dedication to an open-sourced community

All projects are linked to a GitHub. As a community, our projects can only go so far with one person working on it. If you have an interesting project, teaching others about your project is a good start! By helping others understand what you are doing and you work, you can drive more people to help you on your journey.

The tools to do it right

We offer a variety of tools and ways to express your project. It is easy to get started!  Just upload your code and let the intuitive process guide you.
